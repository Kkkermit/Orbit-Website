-  Introducing Orbit.exe  -

Welcome Orbit, a general purpose discord bot designed for handling all servers with easy moderation allowing ease of use for admins and maintaining a user friendly system, Orbit allows easy moderation through its use of an auto moderation system, mod panel and other various commands desinged for upholding all types of moderation within a server. As well as this, Orbit also has extensive community commands that range from mini-games such as /wordle and /2048 to fun community commands such as an inbuilt dictionary and a /image generate command allowing createtivity to flow through servers. Orbit also features a welcoming system along with a levelling system allowing users to level up within your server depending on how messages they have sent. Invite orbit to your server today to bring your commity closer together and allow simple easy moderation throughout your server(s)
